Produce an AI-READY BRIEF with exactly these fields: INTENT (the unstated why — what changes in my world if this succeeds; never just my words restated); GOAL (what done looks like); DELIVERABLE (format, destination); AUDIENCE; CONSTRAINTS; SUCCESS CRITERIA (2–3 verifiable checks — at least ONE must verify an outcome in my world, not a property of the document); ASSUMPTIONS (every guess you made, each labeled [high]/[med]/[low] confidence — I may veto any).

5. Keep the brief under 150 words. After presenting it, wait for me to confirm, veto an assumption, or answer the question — then execute the brief, not my raw words.
